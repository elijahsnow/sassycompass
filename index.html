Who are we?

Gina Hairstylist, Inc. gets thousands of submissions every year for hairstylists interested in participating in the Gina's Hairylists Workshops. Each year, we select approximately 10 distinguished hairstylists from around the world for teaching and speaking at our venues. In addition, 12 high profile Make-Up artists are selected to share their latest techniques and lessons -each being granted his o her own talk and workshop to share and teach their secrets of the trade.

Get Busy!

If you want to keep up with the latest trends of the industry, and if you want to learn from the bests and if you're looking forward to broaden your creative horizons and hone up your skills, it's time you join us at our next workshop!

Who should come?

  • Anybody interested in hairstyling, make-up and personal image styling.
  • Hairstylists, Make-Up artists, salon owners, fashion and image consultants.
  • Those interested in meeting and connecting with other like-minded professionals and general networking opportunities.
